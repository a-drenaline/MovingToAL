Your Complete Alabama Moving Guide

Planning a move to Alabama? This comprehensive guide will help you navigate the process from start to finish.

Before You Move

  • Research your new city thoroughly - climate, cost of living, job market
  • Visit Alabama during different seasons to understand the weather patterns
  • Secure housing - consider renting first before buying
  • Research schools if you have children
  • Transfer medical records and find new healthcare providers

Legal and Administrative Tasks

  • Update your driver's license within 30 days of establishing residency
  • Register to vote in your new county
  • Register your vehicle and get Alabama plates
  • Update voter registration
  • Change address with postal service, banks, and employers
  • Update insurance policies (auto, home, health)

Finding Employment

  • Major industries: Aerospace, automotive, healthcare, agriculture
  • Network through local chambers of commerce
  • Consider remote work opportunities
  • Research salary expectations - cost of living adjustments

Cost Considerations

  • Moving company estimates (get multiple quotes)
  • Temporary lodging if needed
  • Utility deposits and connection fees
  • Emergency fund for unexpected expenses
  • Alabama has no state income tax on military retirement
